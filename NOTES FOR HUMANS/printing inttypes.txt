Basic notes on using inttypes.h

.---------------------------------------,
| type     | printf macro | scanf macro |
|---------------------------------------|
| int8_t   | PRId8        | SCNd8       |
|---------------------------------------|
| int16_t  | PRId16       | SCNd16      |
|---------------------------------------|
| int32_t  | PRId32       | SCNd32      |
|---------------------------------------|
| int64_t  | PRId64       | SCNd64      |
|---------------------------------------|
| uint8_t  | PRIu8        | SCNu8       |
|---------------------------------------|
| uint16_t | PRIu16       | SCNu16      |
|---------------------------------------|
| uint32_t | PRIu32       | SCNu32      |
|---------------------------------------|
| uint64_t | PRIu64       | SCNu64      |
'---------------------------------------'

Example of usage:

int8_t var = 8;

printf("The value is %" PRId8, var);
